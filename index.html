Hi, I'm Merelda

Data Scientist || Electrical Engineer || World Explorer

My passion is in technology – create, analyse and experiment with different tools to shape the way humans interact with each other truly excites me.
I moved to South Africa from Taiwan when I was 17, because of the tremendous language barrier I was forced to relearn everything I thought I knew. Since then, learning, failing, and learn again has determined who I am and what I want to become – a 70-year-old who can claim to have a basic understanding in everything and nothing.
I feel most excited that I can potentially sit in a self-driving car powered by the sun and that the little bits of info we generate every day can be used to change the world.
