With AI, You Either Go Big or Go Home

Most companies choose to start with building small scale PoCs or POVs. Unfortunately, with this approach, many struggled to move from experimentation to scale. According to Michael Gale at the recent Nvidia virtual GTC conference: starting small means staying small.

Companies who enjoy the greatest ROI starts with building their pathway to scale. If you don’t develop with deployment in mind, you will be “stuck in islands of experiments”.

One of the biggest things that keep me up at night is moving from experimentation to production.

Many of our clients shared this sentiment quoted in the article. This shouldn’t even be a worry if the projects are set up with deployment in mind.

Are you building a series of successful but ultimately profitless experiments? Or are you making a radical difference to your organization?

If you’re not sure - let us help you to build with the end in mind.
