Algorithmia: 2020 state of enterprise machine learning


Hello 2020! We started the year by reviewing this white paper published by Algorithmia on the 2020 State of Enterprise Machine Learning.

Here is our quick 2-minute summary:

Over the past 12 months, it is evident that ML adoption is fledgling with a myriad of applications, tools and hardware developments. As a company matures from development to deployment of ML models, the last-mile ML problem presents themselves. The biggest challenges people face in developing ML capabilities are in the deployment, including scaling, version-control of data and models, model reproducibility and aligning stakeholders.
